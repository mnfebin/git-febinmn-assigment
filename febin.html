Anuv Jain

The voice of modern indie music

Bio

Anuv Jain is a singer-songwriter from India. He writes simple, emotional songs that many people love.

Journey

He began writing music in college and shared songs online. Over time his music became popular and he started performing live.

He has released songs like Baarishein and Gul, and is a rising star in indie music.
